Task:

As apart of Information System 101 course in 2015 VuW, We were a project to develop Cyber CV using basic HTML and CSS. We were asked, 'Where do you see yourself in 3 years' time?'

Requirements:

  • Personal statement on the index page.
  • Interest and hobbies section.
  • Education and qualification section.
  • Fictional contact details and reference.

    • Personal objective:

  • Make a minimalistic and professional website.
  • Make a website which represents me.

    • Phase 1 planning the Website.

    Layout: I Choose to use a Horizontal navigation bar and footer because it looks aesthetically pleasing. The content for the page was laid out in a linear structure.

    Colour: It would make sense if I didn't use my favourite colour. Greenery (brighter tone of green), is often associated with nature and has a relaxation effect.
    My accent colour was a lighter tone of blue because it appears to be a colour which does not only look professional but sets a calmness and people trust the colour in a business sense.
    For the text, I used Black and light grey for the background to make the blue and green stand out.

    Font: I used Myriad Pro which is a sans serif font because it both readable in all font sizes and appears to be contemporary.


    Building and testing

    Content: Some of the information in this Cyber CV was not real as it involved a bit of creative forward thinking. My contact details and referees were fictional. Also, my work experience at New World and Xero were also made up, but I did wish I had an internship and job at Xero (Maybe someday soon). A lot of the content was from my college CV with several modifications.
